My Work

My research and personal projects include iPhone programming, productivity software, and extending the uses of the vim editor.

I am the primary developer for the iAcadia iOS application, and developing a website for the Transfer Learning Competition.

I am also a contributor to the UltiSnips plugin for vim, and a developer/maintainer of a few small plugins of my own.

You can find most of my open source work on github.
